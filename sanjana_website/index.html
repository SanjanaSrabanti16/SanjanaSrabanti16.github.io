About Me

PhD Student at University of Illinois at Chicago.

Education

I completed my BSc in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Military Institute of Science and Technology (MIST), Bangladesh in 2018. I am currently pursuing my PhD in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC) under the supervision of Prof. G. Elisabeta (Liz) Marai and Prof. Fabio Miranda. I am part of the Electronic Visualization Laboratory (EVL) working with an amazing group of people.

Work Experience

After completing my undergratuation I worked as a Lecturer of Department of Computer Science at Eastern University, Bangladesh. Since joining UIC in August 2019, I have been working as both a teaching and research assistant.

Research Interest

Data Visualization, Visual Analytics, HCI, UI, Game Design and Development.
